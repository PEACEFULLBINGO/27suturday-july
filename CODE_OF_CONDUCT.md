# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want StudyFlow Orbit to be a welcoming project for students and
contributors of every background and experience level.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, PRs, and discussions.
- Critique code and ideas, not people.
- Assume good faith; ask before assuming malice or incompetence.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks of any kind.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Sustained disruption of discussions or reviews.

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to the maintainers via a private issue or email.
Maintainers will review and respond to all reports, and may warn, temporarily
ban, or permanently ban contributors whose behavior violates this code.

This document is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.1.
